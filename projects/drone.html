Drone

Description

This page is not finished yet but shows some what I have done.

A quadcopter drone with auto-levelling capabilities.
The drone uses four brushless motors with propellers which adjust their thrust in order to keep upright.

Skills

  • Electronics and designing circuits
  • 3D modelling and printing
  • Iterative prototyping
  • Wireless communication

I wanted to find out how to 3D print the most rigid frame for my drone.
Below shows tests where I experimented with print settings to determine which deformed the least when under load.


The data of the print tests.


From the data I calculated a relative score for each test (1 being the best). It is calculated by comparing the weight of the print to how much it deforms.
